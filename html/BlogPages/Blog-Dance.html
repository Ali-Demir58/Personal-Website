Sportif Latin Dance

I enjoy having new experiences. When I was 10, I participated in a folkloric dance show, which was a memorable event.
However, I had never tried Latin dance before. During my first year of university, I became motivated to try Sportive Latin Dance.
It was an enjoyable experience, even though I made many mistakes along the way.
I persevered and continued learning. By the time I reached the second level in the dance course, I participated in a dance show.
Our team worked hard and delivered a well-prepared performance. Unfortunately, due to injuries, I had to leave the dance course.
Additionally, I decided not to pursue a career in dance.
I simply wanted to learn and enjoy the experience.
