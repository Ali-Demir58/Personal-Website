Innovation Boost

I participated in two Innovation Boosts during my two years with the ITU Entrepreneurship Club,
securing 3rd place in both. In the first competition, our healthy living idea was well-received
by health officials and mentors. Afterward, we worked on developing a mobile application to bring
the idea to life. In the second competition, our focus was on digital marketing, and although we earned
3rd place again, we struggled to clearly explain our business model and differentiate ourselves from competitors.
These experiences taught me valuable lessons about generating startup ideas and advancing them through the entrepreneurial journey
