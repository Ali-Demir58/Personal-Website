YETGEN

I participated in the Yetgen 21st Century Competencies and Awareness Program, where I developed essential skills
such as teamwork and presentation techniques, along with technical skills like algorithmic thinking and Excel modeling.
Beyond the core themes of 21st-century literacy in information, technology, finance, and digital realms,
I also explored personal development topics such as mindfulness, stress management, and healthy living. Throughout the Yetgen program,
I formed meaningful friendships and was inspired to both teach and learn continuously.
Being a Yetgen graduate means embracing continuous learning and valuing diverse perspectives.
It involves moving beyond labels, being open-minded, sharing knowledge, taking initiative, and striving for excellence.
In essence, the Yetgen experience instilled in me the importance of lifelong learning and personal growth.
The journey with Yetgen is ongoing and ever-evolving.
