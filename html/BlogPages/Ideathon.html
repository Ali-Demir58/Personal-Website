Ideathon

Ideathon is a competition where participants develop a startup idea within a few hours based on a given theme.
This year's theme was smart cities. Our team conceived the Meta-Yuva project, which involves creating digital twins
of buildings. This allows potential buyers and sellers to obtain detailed information about a property through its digital
twin without needing to visit it physically. Our innovative idea earned us 2nd place. However, it presented challenges due to
the necessity for accurate data. Overall, the event was a great opportunity to make friends and expand my network.
